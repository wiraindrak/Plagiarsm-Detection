Matt Webber is a practicing Farrier in Jefferson County, Missouri .Originally from Moline, Illinois, he moved to Minnesota for a barn manager position in 2003.
He attended Oklahoma State Horseshoeing School at the end of 2004.He has since gone into business for himself and is shoeing full time.
In July, 2006 he will be relocating to Pevely, Missouri, about 20 miles south of Saint Louis.
He is a full service Farrier with experience in Hot, Cold, and Corrective shoeing, as well as trimming and hand-made shoes.

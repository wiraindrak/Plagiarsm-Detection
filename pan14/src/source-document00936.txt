Lawn Seed
Lush Sun 'n' Shade Lawn Seed
A premium quality lawn seed perfect for growing and oversowing lawns in either full sun or in shaded areas. Suitable for high traffic areas and large sections of lawn that are in full sun or semi-shade.
Features
Part of a complete range of premium lawn care products.
Quick to establish and holds colour well in winter.
Grows in full sun and shaded areas.
Best sown in autumn or early spring.
Ingredients
25% – Fine Turf Type Perennial Rye
Precautions
To ensure best results keep pets, particularly dogs or other large animals, off area until grass is established. Take extra care with dogs as grass seeds can become lodged in paws or ears and cause problems. Lawn seed won’t germinate if it is constantly disturbed by people or dogs.

Four underwater crossings in the Hampton Roads area:
The mid-town and downtown Elizabeth River tunnels
The Hampton Roads Bridge-Tunnel on Interstate 64
The Monitor-Merrimac Memorial Bridge-Tunnel on Interstate 664
Two mountain tunnels on Interstate 77 in southwest Virginia: East River and Big Walker
Three toll roads:
Northern Virginia's Dulles Toll Road
The Powhite Parkway Extension near Richmond
The Pocahontas Parkway near Richmond

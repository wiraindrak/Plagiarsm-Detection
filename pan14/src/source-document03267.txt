Vision and Mission
The Genesis Faraday Partnership is that:Vision
The rapid expansion in knowledge of animal genetics will provide unprecedented opportunities for the improvement of animal health, animal welfare and animal production and hence sustainability of farmed livestock species.
Our is that:Mission
The Genesis Faraday Partnership will improve the interaction between the research community, intermediate organisations and the animal health and animal breeding industries to ensure that these industries are competitively placed to exploit the opportunities that arise from the iological revolution
The Genesis Faraday Partnership was established in January 2003.
